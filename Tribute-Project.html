Tony Stark

The man who saved a billion lives

Here's a time line of Tony Stark's life:

  • 1970 - Tony Stark is born
  • 1983 -Tony Hacks into the Pentagon on a dare from his friends
  • 1987 -Tony Graduates Summa Cum Laude from MIT
  • 1991 -Tony loses his parents to an alleged car crash
  • 1992 -Tony takes over Stark Industries
  • 1999 -Tony blows off several genius scientist to hook up with Maya Hansen
  • 2009 -Tony is taken prisoner after demonstrating the Jericho prototype, eventually escpaing after creating a makeshift battle suit
  • 2009 -Tony fights his business mentor to protect his arc reactor technology
  • 2010 -Tony prevents a drone attack at the stark expo organized by the son of one of Howard Stark's Rivals
  • 2012 -Tony units the Avengers to stop Loki's Chituri incursion of Earth
  • 2012 -Tony stops Aldrich Killian from assassinating the President
  • 2015 -Tony creates Ultron in a bid to protect the world from future onslaughts, and this results in the Battle of Sokovia
  • 2016 -Tony Fights Captain America and his allies over surrendering Bucky Barnes and the signing of the Sokovia Accords
  • 2017 -Tony mentors Peter Parkers, saves a bunch of people on the Staten Island Ferry, and gets engaged to Pepper Potts
  • 2018 -Tony and the Avengers fail to stop Thanos from uniting the infinity stones
  • 2018 -Tony returns to Earth after Captain Marvel rescues him
  • 2019 -Tony becomes a father after Pepper gives birth to Morgan Stark
  • 2023 -Tony solves time travel, helps orchestrate the infinity stone time heist and snaps Thanos out of existence, perishing from the strain on his body

Works Cited: A Complete Timeline Of Tony Stark, The Most Important Person In The MCU by T.W.Mitchell
